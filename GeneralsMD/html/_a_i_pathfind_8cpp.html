◆ dont_forceRefreshCalling

#define dont_forceRefreshCalling

Calculate zones. A zone is an area of the same terrain - clear, water or cliff. The utility of zones is that if current location and destiontion are in the same zone, you can successfully pathfind. If you are a multiple terrain vehicle, like amphibious transport, the lookup is a little more complicated.
